WHO ARE WE?

We are a group of childhood friends who have a passion for good brewing and fancy beers. We boys, Michael, Piere and Fredrick, enjoy educating our selves as well as others on beer-brewing, and, ofcourse, drinking beer!

OUR HISTORY

In 2010 our brewery became a reality. Since then we have produced over 100 different batches and held over 500 courses on different topics in beer brewing. With inspiration to learn more and educate more people, we started this blog, so that we have a good platform to easily convey our passion

Thanks to Daniel Ekerhovd for creating this blog for us!
